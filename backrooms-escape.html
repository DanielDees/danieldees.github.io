    NOCLIP

    ESCAPE THE BACKROOMS · LEVEL 0

    You weren't paying attention, and you fell through the world. Now you're here: damp carpet, yellowed wallpaper, the endless drone of fluorescent light. Roughly six hundred million square miles of nothing.

    Something else fell through, a long time ago. It is still here. Find what you need, restore the power, and locate the exit door — quietly.

    headphones recommended · click DESCEND to begin

    HOW TO PLAY

    FIELD NOTES — DO NOT LOSE

    CONTROLS

    • WASD move · MOUSE look
    • SHIFT sprint — drains stamina and makes noise
    • C crouch / hide — slow but silent
    • E interact (pick up items, use panels & doors)
    • H this guide · ESC pause / release mouse

    OBJECTIVES

    Complete them in order. Collect 3 bottles of almond water, then find the fuse, then restore power at the breaker panel. Only then will the exit door unseal. Your progress is tracked in the top-left log.

    THE ENTITY

    It wakes when you start taking things. It mostly wanders — but it sees far down straight corridors, and it hears sprinting from a long way off. If the lights around you begin to flicker hard and you hear it breathing, it is close.

    HOW TO HIDE

    Break its line of sight — put walls and pillars between you — then hold C to crouch and stay still. Crouched and unseen, you are nearly invisible: it will stalk to where it last saw you, search, give up, and move on. Never try to outrun it in a straight hallway for long; your stamina will fail before its patience does. Corner, crouch, wait, breathe.
